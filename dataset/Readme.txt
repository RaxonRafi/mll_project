Title: Empirical Survey Data on the Determinants of Youth Migration Decisions and Psychological Stress in Bangladesh

Authors: Sajib Biswas, Md. Abbas Ali Khan
Date: 2025

Description:
This dataset contains survey responses from 1,614 Bangladeshi youth regarding their migration intentions, psychological stress factors, and coping strategies. The data was collected between January and April 2025 using a stratified convenience sampling method.

Files included:
1. Cleaned_Youth_Migration_Data.csv: The raw survey data with standardized variable names.
2. Data_Dictionary.csv: A codebook explaining the variable names and corresponding survey questions.
3. Survey_Questionnaire.pdf: The blank questionnaire used for data collection.

Missing Values:
Missing values are marked as empty cells or 'NaN'.
